# LIPU Design Vision

This website must not resemble a traditional Indian UPVC business website.

We are not selling windows.

We are selling home transformation.

Inspiration:

* Pella Windows
* Andersen Windows
* REHAU
* Apple
* Tesla
* Airbnb

Design Principles:

1. Large immersive hero sections
2. Architectural photography
3. Storytelling before products
4. Full-width layouts
5. Minimalist typography
6. High whitespace
7. Premium feel
8. Smooth animations
9. Project-first navigation
10. Mobile-first design

Avoid:

* Product catalog homepage
* Generic hero banner
* Corporate stock photos
* Cluttered menus
* Bright gradients
* Cheap animations

Homepage Flow:

Hero
↓
Home Transformation
↓
Featured Projects
↓
Before / After Experiences
↓
Design Inspiration
↓
AI Home Visualizer
↓
Product Discovery
↓
Testimonials
↓
Quote Request

Users should feel:

"I want my home to look like this."

before they ever browse products.

Products are secondary.

Transformation is primary.
