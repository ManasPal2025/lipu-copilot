# Window Design for Apartments

## Facade harmony

Match society exterior colour rules — usually white or beige exterior, woodgrain interior optional. Consistent sill height line across balcony improves tower appearance.

## Living room

Sliding door plus sidelight fixed panel common; consider acoustic upgrade if road-facing. Avoid mixing too many grid patterns on one facade.

## Bedrooms

Casement with mesh for road side; smaller ventilator in attached bath. Restrictors for upper floors with children.

## Space tips

Sliding saves space versus casement in compact 3 BHK; use casement only where seal priority justifies swing clearance.
