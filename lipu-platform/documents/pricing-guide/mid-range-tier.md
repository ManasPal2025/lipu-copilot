# Mid-Range UPVC Windows

## Typical scope

70 mm branded profile, double glazed standard, branded multi-point hardware, white and woodgrain options, proper drainage and installation protocol.

## Price range

Bedroom casement 4×4 ft approximately ₹15,000–22,000; balcony slider 6×7 ft ₹25,000–40,000 installed.

## Value proposition

Best balance of comfort, longevity, and cost for typical 3 BHK apartment or villa. Handles monsoon and AC load well when glass matched to exposure.

## Upgrade hooks

Add acoustic laminated glass on road side, mesh, restrictors — incremental at order time.
