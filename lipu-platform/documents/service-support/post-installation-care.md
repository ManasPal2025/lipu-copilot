# Post-Installation Care Guide

## First 30 days

Normal slight settling may occur — report stiff operation promptly while warranty active. Do not paint over frames or seal weep holes during home finishing.

## Cleaning

Mild soap, soft cloth, vacuum tracks. No abrasives or thinners on lamination.

## Seasonal

May pre-monsoon service; post-monsoon inspection; coastal monthly rinse.

## Documentation

Keep warranty card, invoice, and hardware brand record for future service orders.
