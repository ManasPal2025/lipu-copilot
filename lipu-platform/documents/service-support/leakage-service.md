# Leakage Investigation and Repair Service

## Diagnosis

Technician traces water path — sill, meeting rail, wall junction, or overhead chhajja. Hose test reproduces leak when intermittent.

## Common fixes

Roller adjustment, gasket replacement, external silicone renewal, drainage clearing, wall crack referral. Interior silicone alone rarely correct fix.

## Warranty

Install-related leaks within workmanship period covered; civil or user-caused exclusions apply.

## Prevention visit

Annual pre-monsoon check cheaper than emergency August leak call.
