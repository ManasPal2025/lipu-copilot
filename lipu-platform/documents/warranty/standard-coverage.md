# Standard Warranty Coverage

LIPU warranties are issued per project after installation completion and handover. Coverage applies to **manufacturing defects** and **installation workmanship** under normal residential use in Odisha climate conditions.

## Profile and frame

UPVC profiles are covered against manufacturing defects such as warping, chamber failure, or colour inconsistency under normal use for the period stated on your warranty certificate (typically **10 years** on profile, project-specific terms apply).

## Hardware

Handles, locks, hinges, and rollers are covered against premature failure due to manufacturing defects — commonly **10 years** on standard hardware packages. Coastal installations may include upgraded fittings with terms noted on your certificate.

## Installation workmanship

Sealing, fixing, and alignment issues attributable to installation are covered for a defined period after handover (commonly **1 year**). Report suspected leaks promptly after monsoon seasons so we can inspect while conditions are recent.

## Glass

Glass is covered separately for seal failure (misting between panes) and manufacturing defects. Accidental breakage, scratches, and impact damage are **not** covered — see exclusions document.

## Warranty certificate

Your signed handover pack includes warranty start date, covered openings list, and contact details. Keep this with your property documents.
