# Installation Handover

Handover marks the start of your warranty period and confirms each opening operates correctly.

## At handover you receive

- Walkthrough of each opening — open, close, lock demonstration
- Warranty certificate with covered items and dates
- Opening schedule listing sizes and glass types installed
- Basic care leaflet (track cleaning, hardware lubrication)
- Support contact for future service

## We check together

- Smooth operation of sashes and rollers
- Lock engagement on all points
- No visible gaps when closed
- Drainage holes clear in tracks
- Interior and exterior sealant continuity

## First 30 days

New frames may settle microscopically; minor adjustment is normal. Report stiffness, rattling, or leaks promptly — early adjustment is faster than waiting through a full monsoon.

## Long-term care in Odisha climate

- Clean tracks before monsoon and after heavy pollen seasons
- Avoid abrasive cleaners on UPVC — use mild soap and water
- Do not paint UPVC profiles
- Schedule optional annual service visits in coastal Puri properties
