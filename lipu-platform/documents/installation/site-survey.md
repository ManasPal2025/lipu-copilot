# Site Survey and Measurement

Every LIPU project begins with accurate measurement. Online estimates and wizard recommendations are starting points only — **final pricing follows site survey**.

## What we measure

- Width, height, and depth of each opening
- Lintel and sill condition
- Wall material (brick, concrete, AAC block)
- Obstructions (grills, AC units, plumbing)
- Drainage and overhang for monsoon exposure

## What we discuss on site

- Glass type per room orientation
- Colour and handle preferences
- Timeline and access for installation crew
- Scaffolding or lift requirements for high-rise Bhubaneswar apartments

## Survey duration

A typical 3-bedroom flat survey takes **45–90 minutes**. Larger villas or partial renovations may take half a day.

## After the survey

You receive a written quote with line items per opening. Production begins after booking confirmation and advance payment as per your project agreement.
