# Triple Glazed Glass

Triple glazed units add a third pane and second air gap, improving insulation beyond standard double glazing. Use selectively on **high sun load** or **high noise** openings rather than every window in the home.

## Best applications

- West-facing living rooms on upper floors in Bhubaneswar
- Home theatres or bedrooms under flight paths (where applicable)
- Large fixed picture windows where ventilation is handled elsewhere

## Trade-offs

- Higher cost and frame weight
- Slightly reduced light transmission vs thinner units
- Longer lead time for custom IGU sizes

## When double glazing is enough

For most Odisha bedrooms and balconies, quality **double glazed** units with the right coating deliver excellent comfort at lower cost. Triple glazing is recommended when survey notes severe exposure or explicit acoustic requirements.
