# Double Glazed Glass

Double glazed units (IGU) use two panes of glass separated by a spacer and sealed air or argon gap. This is the **most common choice** for LIPU installations in Bhubaneswar and across Odisha.

## How it helps

- Reduces heat transfer compared to single glass — important for sunny rooms
- Improves sound reduction from traffic and neighbourhood noise
- Reduces condensation on the inner pane in air-conditioned rooms
- Moderate cost increase over single glazing with strong everyday benefits

## Typical configuration

A common residential build-up is **5 mm clear outer + 12 mm air gap + 5 mm inner**. Laminated or low-E coatings can be added to one pane for enhanced performance.

## Best rooms for double glazing

- Road-facing bedrooms and living rooms
- Home offices in apartment towers
- Any room where you run AC regularly
- Balconies converted to enclosed sit-outs

## Maintenance

IGU seals are factory-made and should not be drilled or modified on site. If mist appears **between** the panes, the unit may have failed and needs replacement — covered under glass warranty terms when applicable.
