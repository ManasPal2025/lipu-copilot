# Handles and Hinges

## Handles

Inline versus crank-style; keyed versus non-keyed; colour matching profile. Handle drives lock gearbox — quality handle means smooth long operation.

## Hinges

Two to four hinges per casement sash based on height and weight. Adjustable hinges correct minor sag; branded hinges rated for sash kg.

## Coastal note

Stainless or special coating on coastal projects — standard zinc hinges rust and stain white frames.

## Replacement

Handles and hinges are service parts — available long after install if brand documented.
