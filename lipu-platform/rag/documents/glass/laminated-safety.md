# Laminated Safety Glass

Laminated glass bonds two or more panes with a PVB interlayer. If broken, fragments adhere to the interlayer instead of falling — improving **safety** and **security**.

## When homeowners choose laminated glass

- Ground-floor windows facing lanes or parking areas
- French doors and entrance glazing
- Homes where children play near large glass panels
- Noise reduction on busy streets (laminated layers damp vibration)

## Combined with double glazing

Laminated glass can form one pane of an insulated double glazed unit — you get safety and thermal benefits together. This is a popular upgrade for Bhubaneswar apartment ground floors.

## Cost note

Laminated options cost more than standard float glass. We recommend them selectively on vulnerable openings rather than every window in the house.
