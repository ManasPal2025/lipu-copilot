# Pre-Installation Checklist

Good preparation keeps installation safe, fast, and clean.

## One week before

- Confirm installation dates with our coordinator
- Clear furniture **at least 1 metre** from each opening inside and outside where possible
- Remove fragile items from walls near windows being replaced
- Inform building security and elevator society (common in Bhubaneswar apartments)
- Arrange parking or material unloading space for our vehicle

## Day of installation

- Ensure an adult is present for access decisions
- Keep pets and children away from work areas
- Provide power point access for tools
- Have old grills or shutters removed **only if** agreed in advance — otherwise our team advises on sequence

## During monsoon season

If installation falls between June and September in Odisha:

- We may reschedule if cyclone warnings are active
- Interior finishing may be staged to avoid open walls during cloudbursts
- Temporary covering is used where openings must remain open overnight

## After each room

Our team cleans coarse debris daily. Final deep cleaning of your home is your choice — we leave openings ready for normal use.
