# Warranty Exclusions

Understanding exclusions helps set correct expectations and avoid preventable damage.

## Not covered

- **Accidental impact** — cricket balls, furniture strikes, forced break-in damage
- **Misuse** — hanging heavy loads on handles, blocking tracks with debris
- **Unauthorized modifications** — drilling into frames, replacing glass by non-LIPU technicians
- **Natural disasters** — cyclone, flood, or earthquake damage beyond normal weather
- **Normal wear** — minor scratches on tracks from regular use, fading of external paint on walls adjacent to frames
- **Neglected maintenance** — blocked drainage holes causing water overflow into interiors

## Maintenance is your responsibility

Homeowners should:

- Vacuum or brush tracks seasonally, especially before monsoon
- Lubricate hardware once a year with approved silicone spray (not oil that attracts dust)
- Avoid pressure washing directly into lock mechanisms

## Third-party work

If you alter openings structurally (wall demolition, lintel changes) after LIPU installation, warranty on affected units may be void unless we re-inspect and re-certify.
