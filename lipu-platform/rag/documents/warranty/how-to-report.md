# How to Report an Issue

We want issues resolved quickly and fairly. Follow these steps after installation.

## Step 1 — Gather details

Before contacting us, note:

- Which room and opening (e.g. master bedroom balcony sliding)
- When the problem started
- Photos or a short video showing the issue (water trail, stiff handle, misted glass)
- Your warranty certificate or project reference if available

## Step 2 — Contact support

Use the **Customer Support** form on our website or call the service number on your handover document. Provide your name, phone number, product type, and a clear description.

## Step 3 — Site visit if needed

Our team may schedule an inspection — often within **one business day** in Bhubaneswar and Cuttack, slightly longer for distant Odisha districts. Monsoon peak periods may have higher scheduling volume.

## Step 4 — Resolution

Valid warranty claims are repaired or replaced per coverage terms. Issues outside warranty receive a transparent quote before work proceeds.

## Emergency leaks during monsoon

If active water ingress occurs during heavy rain, send photos immediately and use temporary towels or buckets to limit interior damage. We prioritise active leak calls where safety or property damage risk is high.
